about us

nana's green tea is a modern japanese cafe specializing in green teas and japanese foods and desserts delivered in a modern fashion. we are a global matcha brand originating from tokyo, japan. we incorporate traditional japanese ingredients such as matcha, azuki, warabimochi in new, creative ways of presentation. if you have never had matcha or never liked the matcha you have tried before, give us a chance to change your mind! looking forward to serving you soon!

a place for japanese delicacies

we blend a variety of teas to make our original matcha green tea using our flair for blending and techniques that date back to the Edo period. we carefully elect ingredients that are sourced from all over japan.
